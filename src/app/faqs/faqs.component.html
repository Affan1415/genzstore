Frequently Asked Questions

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping. Please note that shipping times and fees may vary depending on your location.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may return it for a full refund or exchange.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method you choose at checkout. Typically, orders are processed and shipped within 1-2 business days.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or on the carrier's website.

Do you offer free shipping?

We offer free shipping on all orders over $50 within the United States.

What if my order is damaged or defective?

If your order arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order and we will be happy to assist you.

Can I cancel or change my order after it has been placed?

We cannot guarantee that changes or cancellations can be made after an order has been placed. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes to your order.

Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes, we offer various discounts and promotions throughout the year. Sign up for our email newsletter to stay up-to-date on our latest offers.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by emailing support@yourstore.com or by using the contact form on our website. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
